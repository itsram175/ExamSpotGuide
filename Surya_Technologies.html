About Center

Surya Technologies is located at Peerjadiguda. It is a Computer based Test (CBT) Exam Centre. Many Competitive and Entrance Exams will be conducted here. The centre provides you the clock room facility to store your belongings safe. There is parking facility at the centre, but you need to arrive early to avail that facility. If you come late the parking space will be filled and they wont allow you to park your vehicle to park inside. And the exam centre will not provide any facilities for parents or guardians. The nearest city bus stop to this center is Canara Nagar bus Stop. After getting down at Canara Nagar Bus Stop, cross the road and walk in reverse direction for less than 200 meters. You can find the exam centre. You will find many buses to Green Park Colony from various important Bus Stations and Railway Stations of Hyderabad. How to Reach From?

SECUNDERABAD

Railways Station

If you are in Secunderabad Station, come out and walk to Rethifile Bus Stop. Take the bus with the Number 280 or 280B and get down at Canara Nagar Bus Stop.

KACHEGUDA

Railways Station

If you are in Kacheguda Station, walk to the bus station, opposite to the station. Take the bus with the Number 3KN and get down at Habsiguda Bus Stop. Now at same bus stop take 280 or 280B and get down at Canara Nagar Bus Stop.

MGBS

MG Bus Station

If you are in Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station, walk to the city Bus Station (CBS). Take the bus with Number 3KN and get down at Habsiguda Bus Stop. Now at same bus stop take 280 or 280B and get down at Canara Nagar Stop.

JBS

Jubliee Bus Station

If you are in Jubliee Bus Station, come out and take the bus with the Number 90L, 279 or 290 and get down at Alugadda Bhavi Bus Stop. Now at same bus stop take 280 or 280B and get down at Canara Nagar Bus Stop.
