About Center

ION Digital Zone 1 & 2 Mallapur, Hyderabad is located at Mallapur near Noma Function Hall. It is a Computer based Test (CBT) Exam Centre operated by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). Many Competitive and Entrance Exams will be conducted here. The centre provides you the clock room facility to store your belongings safe. There is parking facility at the centre, but you need to arrive early to avail that facility. If you come late the parking space will be filled and they wont allow you to park your vehicle to park inside. And the exam centre will not provide any facilities for parents or guardians. This exam centres is located beside Noma Function hall and Noma Talkies. The nearest city bus stop to this center is Noma Function Hall. After Getting down at Noma Function Hall you have to walk in reverse direction for approximately 300 meters. You can find many buses to Noma Function Hall from various important Bus Stations and Railway Stations of Hyderabad. Scroll down to know the detailed bus route in simple words.

How to Reach From?

SECUNDERABAD

Railways Station

If you are in Secunderabad Station, come out and walk to blue sea hotel bus stop. Take the bus with the Number 250, 250C or 49M/250 and get down at Noma Function Hall Bus Stop.

KACHEGUDA

Railways Station

If you are in Kacheguda Station, walk to the bus station which is opposite to the station. Take the bus with the Number 3KN and get down at Noma Function Hall Bus Stop.

MGBS

MG Bus Station

If you are in Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station, walk to the city Bus Station (CBS). Take the bus with the Number 3KN and get down at Noma Function Hall Bus Stop.

JBS

Jubliee Bus Station

If you are in Jubliee Bus Station, come out and take the bus with the Number 90L, 279 or 290 and get down at Alugadda Bhavi Bus Stop. Now at same bus stop take 250, 250C or 49M/250 and get down at Noma Function Hall.
